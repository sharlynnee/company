Saul Goodman

Ceo & Founder

We're the definitive bank for businesses in Nigeria, and their customers. Millions of people across the country depend on our products to grow their businesses, and power their personal finance.

Sara Wilsson

Designer

Our initial approach to delivering financial happiness was creating financial solutions aimed at optimising the operations of partner banks and financial institutions. These solutions were deployed to 95% of Nigerian banks and improved the service experience for over 10 million of their customers

Jena Karlis

Store Owner

We decided to take a more direct approach to achieve our mission. Using our industry experience, technical depth and customer-first approach, we started providing business-facing products to merchants. We offered financial solutions to merchants selling cash (agents) through our products - Moniepoint and Monnify.

Matt Brandon

Freelancer

With Moniepoint, we built Nigeria’s strongest distribution network for financial services. We became the leading agency banking solution in Nigeria, processing up to $7 billion monthly in over 120,000,000 transactions each month. Moniepoint became the focal point for financial services in areas where traditional banking was inaccessible. We also offered solutions to merchants selling goods and services who needed to receive payments online through our product- Monnify, processing over $22 million daily in 500,000 transactions.

John Larson

Entrepreneur

Emily Harison

Store Owner

Our solutions became widespread, and we hit a milestone of over 3 million businesses using our products. In August 2023, we launched a Personal banking solution, to extend our reliable infrastructure to the customers and employees of our businesses. With this, we provided extensive support to our merchants, enabling seamless payments from their customers, and a more efficient overall financial experience.
